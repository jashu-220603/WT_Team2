Frequently Asked Questions

Check the Assigned Cases section on your dashboard. Prioritize cases marked as "High". Visit the location, gather necessary materials, and coordinate with your team to address the issue. Open the complaint and update its status appropriately.

Click the "Update" button next to any active complaint on your dashboard. A modal will appear where you can select the new status (e.g., In Progress, Solved) and add remarks regarding the action taken.

Priority is generally assigned automatically by the system or the Admin during triage, based on factors like safety risks, traffic impact, and the number of similar complaints in the area.

If you find a complaint to be a duplicate or physically invalid upon inspection, status it as "Resolved/Solved" and provide clear notes in the remarks specifying why it is invalid so the Admin and citizen are notified.

Use the Contact Admin page from your sidebar menu to fill out the contact form or view direct email and phone number details.

Analytics automatically gather data from the complaints assigned to you. The system compiles your resolved vs pending ratio and tracks resolution times over the month to display in chart format on the Analytics page.

Complaint tracking lets you input a specific Complaint ID and visually see its journey through the workflow steps (Assigned, Pending, In Progress, Solved), helping you quickly check current state.
