Frequently Asked Questions

✖

1. How can I register a complaint?

Click on the File Complaint button and fill the complaint form.

2. How can I track my complaint?

Use the Track Complaint option and enter your complaint ID.

3. How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Most complaints are resolved within 3-7 working days.

4. Can I upload evidence with my complaint?

Yes, you can upload documents, photos, or proof while submitting a complaint.

5. Who reviews my complaint?

The complaint is first reviewed by admin and then assigned to an officer.
