Help / FAQ

Common questions and answers.

How to file a complaint?

Navigate to "File Complaint" from the sidebar, fill in your details, select category, upload evidence, and submit. You will receive a Complaint ID.

How to track a complaint?

Go to "Track Complaint", enter your CMP ID, or view it directly from the "Your Complaints" tab.

How long does complaint resolution take?

Resolution times vary based on severity. Average is 24-72 hours.

How can I contact support?

Use the "Contact Support" tab in the sidebar to find our helpline and email details.
